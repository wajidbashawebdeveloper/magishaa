ABOUT MAGISHAA CONSTRUCTION

With an enduring presence in Chennai’s construction landscape, Magishaa Construction has become synonymous with refined quality and exceptional craftsmanship.

Our portfolio spans bespoke residences, premium apartments, luxury villas, and commercial developments each shaped by meticulous planning, advanced engineering, and a passion for design. Over the past decade, we have successfully delivered 500+ projects, spanning an impressive 27,13,000 sq. ft of completed residential and commercial spaces. We have built trust, transparency, and lasting relationships with every client who chooses us.

Our commitment to on-time delivery, ethical practices, and superior build quality positions. Our delivery framework is powered by integrated project management systems, predictive planning tools, and strict milestone tracking. With controlled workflows, resource optimization, and proactive risk management, Magishaa ensures that project timelines remain uncompromised. The brand is credited for being one of the very few developers with end-to-end capabilities, right from project inception to finish. It has an enviable reputation for being a transparent business house, with sound emphasis on quality and commitment towards honouring promises.
